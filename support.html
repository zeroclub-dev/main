Frequently Asked Questions

View All FAQs

How do I connect my domain to my Zero Club website?

To connect your domain, go to "My Websites" in the dashboard, select the website you want to connect, and click "Domain Settings." From there, you can either purchase a new domain or connect an existing one. If connecting an existing domain, you'll need to update your DNS settings at your domain registrar to point to our servers. We provide step-by-step instructions for all major domain registrars.

What ad networks work best with Zero Club templates?

All Zero Club templates are designed to work seamlessly with major ad networks including Google AdSense, Mediavine, Adthrive, Ezoic, and more. Our templates have optimized ad placements built-in that maximize revenue while maintaining good user experience. We recommend starting with Google AdSense for new sites, and then applying to premium networks like Mediavine or Adthrive once you reach 50,000+ monthly sessions.

How do I implement the TikTok traffic strategy?

Our TikTok traffic strategy is covered in detail in the "TikTok Traffic Explosion Masterclass" course. The basic approach involves creating short, high-value content that solves specific problems in your niche, using trending sounds and hooks, and including a clear call-to-action that directs viewers to your website. You should aim to post consistently (at least 3-5 times per week) and analyze your performance to see which content drives the most traffic.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Simply go to "Account Settings" and select "Subscription Management." From there, you can cancel your subscription with just a few clicks. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer refunds for partial months, but you're welcome to use all the features until your subscription expires.

How long does it typically take to start making money?

The timeline to monetization varies depending on your niche, the traffic strategies you implement, and the effort you put in. Based on our member data, most members see their first revenue within 2-4 weeks using our rapid traffic strategies (like TikTok and Pinterest). To reach significant income ($1,000+ per month), most successful members take 3-6 months of consistent effort. The key is to follow our monetization strategies from day one and implement traffic generation techniques consistently.

What's the difference between the monthly and annual plans?

The monthly plan gives you access to our core features at $19.99 per month. The annual plan costs $49.99 per year (saving you 79% compared to the monthly plan) and includes additional premium features like the TikTok Traffic Masterclass, Advanced Affiliate Marketing training, premium website templates, AI content generation tools, and monthly group coaching calls. For most members, the annual plan provides the best value and includes all the resources needed to build a successful website business.
